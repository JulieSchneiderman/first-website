JASON SILVA

Origins: The Journey of Humankind - a new National Geographic series
March 26, 2017

A few years ago I stumbled upon Jason Silva back when the National Geographic show Brain Games had just come out. I had never really understood the power of my brain or better yet, how often it plays tricks on me. Jason speaks with such enthusiasm that I couldn’t help but binge watch his show and thus, my interest into the world of cognitive science began. I strongly encourage everyone to give one of these episodes a try. The show is designed in such a way that you don’t even realize you’re learning, and trust me you’ll be shocked at how many times your eyes widen and you mouth “wooaahhh.” And don’t worry; it's available on Netflix.

Anyway, I’ve been following Jason’s career for some time now. Watching his vlogs on a regular basis and reading through the articles he posts on his Facebook page. Luckily for me, he’s come out with a new show for me to secretly obsess over. It’s called “Origin: The Journey of Humankind. ” It’s the story of how the world became modern and the path we’re taking into the future.

The video above is the episode I’m most looking forward too.

Give it a watch.

- J


PURPOSE

Welcome to the life of J, March 27, 2017

This is a learning experience so bare with me.

I've learned a lot about computing this year and it's time to let that new found knowledge speak for itself.

The idea of this page is to learn new skills and express myself here. To post current and past projects and track my progress live for the world to see. So that maybe in 20 years when I'm (hopefully) running the world, I'll be able to look back and see how far I've come, kind of like a virtual scrapbook. I'm really hoping for this website to push me to continue to work on my own projects and not let schoolwork drag me down and break my spirits. My grades don't define me, nor do anyone else's for that matter. But my passion does.

- J
